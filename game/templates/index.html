An Oil Trading Sim Built By Oil Traders


Learn how to make two way prices. React to news information. Trade against an AI. Develop your understanding of oil trading.



How It Works


  • Objective: Trade oil effectively within a 3-minute round to make a profit by predicting the final price based on real-time news.
  • Starting Point: Each round begins with an initial oil price.
  • News Interpretation: Analyze news stories released during the round to forecast the closing price of oil.
  • Trading Mechanics: Make bids (buy price) and offers (sell price) to trade against an AI opponent.
  • Bid and Offer: Your bid is the maximum you're willing to pay; your offer is the minimum you're willing to sell for.
  • AI Competition: Trade effectively against the AI, which also makes its own bids and offers.
  • Lifting the Offer: You can choose to 'lift the offer'—accepting the AI's sell price—if it benefits your strategy.
  • Round Conclusion: Profit is determined by the difference between your trade price and the final revealed price at the end of the round.
